Easy Broccoli Chow Mein

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Egg noodles
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce

Recipe

  1. Chop up the broccoli and add to a pan with oil.
  2. Add a small amount of both dark and light soy sauce to the broccoli. Cook for 5-10mins.
  3. Prepare the noodles from the packet instructions. If no need for boiling add straight to the pan.
  4. Add the remaining soy sauces and allow to cook for 2 mins.
  5. Serve on its own or with a side of prawn crackers.
