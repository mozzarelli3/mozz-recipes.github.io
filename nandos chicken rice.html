Nando's Chicken Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast/pieces
  • Nando's sauce
  • Rice
  • Tenderstem broccoli
  • Garlic oil (or garlic granules and olive oil)

Recipe

  1. Start by slicing the chicken breast into thin pieces. Coat generously in Nando's sauce and place in oven for 30 mins.
  2. While the chicken is cooking, chop up the broccoli and boil for 6 mins.
  3. Cook the rice for 10-12 mins.
  4. Once the broccoli is done, coat in the garlic oil.
  5. Allow all to cool in the fridge until cold.
  6. Serve in a bowl, with rice then the chicken and broccoli on top.
