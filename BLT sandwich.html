BLT Sandwich

Bacon, Turkey and Lettuce Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Sliced turkey
  • Bacon strips
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Nice quality bread (maybe a ciabatta or similar)

Recipe

  1. Start by cooking the bacon in a pan until almost crispy. While this is cooking, butter the bread.
  2. Add the cooked bacon onto the bread.
  3. Next place the lettuce pieces on top of the bacon.
  4. Evenly spread the pre-cooked turkey slices on top of the lettuce.
  5. Add the final slice of buttered bread and you're done!
